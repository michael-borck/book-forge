Three steps to a book

Describe

Start with a topic

Type what the book should be about, pick a style — educational, technical, narrative — and choose how many chapters you want.

Forge

Watch it take shape

Two-phase generation builds the structure first, then writes each chapter with a live streaming preview and real-time token and cost tracking.

Export

Take it anywhere

Ship the finished book as clean Markdown, styled HTML, or a print-ready PDF, straight from your library.

Bring your own AI

Any major provider

Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini, Groq — or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. Your key, your model, one settings tab.

Or fully local

Point BookForge at Ollama and generate entire books without a single token leaving your machine.

Keys kept safe

API keys are encrypted with your operating system's secure storage and never leave your device except to call the provider you chose.
